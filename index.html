About Us

Digging for Shovels is a Boston-based indie rock/reggae group playing original songs and unique covers. We offer high energy, plugged in performances and we also play acoustic for smaller venues. Our music blends catchy rock riffs mixed with rap, ska and reggae.

Meet the Band

Rich Pin

Guitar and Vocals


A guitar player for over 20 years, Rich loves playing and creating music. His influences include rock, reggae, pop, grunge, punk, metal and ska.

Jamison Nicosia

Drums and Vocals


A drummer spanning multiple decades, Jamison has seen it all. Playing punk, ska, rock or hip-hop, Jamison is comfortable keeping time in any genre.
