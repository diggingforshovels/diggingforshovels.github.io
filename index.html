Who We Are

Digging for Shovels is a Boston-based indie rock duo consisting of guitarist/vocalist Rich Pin and drummer Jamison Nicosia. Our music blends catchy melodies with heartfelt lyrics, creating an immersive listening experience.

Our Music

We have released several singles and EPs that showcase our unique sound. Our music is available on all major streaming platforms, including Spotify, Apple Music, and YouTube. Check out our latest releases and enjoy the journey with us!

Upcoming Shows

We regularly perform at local venues and festivals around Boston. Stay tuned for our upcoming shows and events by following us on social media or checking our website for the latest updates.
